About Me

West side Cincinnati born and raised. Married my soul mate and followed him to Silicon Valley to live the dream. Stayed for 15+ years during which we had 4 amazing kiddos. Came back home to Cincinnati in the summer of 2023. Still living the dream!

I believe in the power of home. The goal of an interior designer should be to create a pleasing environment where clients feel good about themselves and life in general.
