Design Coaching

If you’ve ever wanted professional insights on your design challenges, consider a coaching appointment. Remodeling and New Construction Design

Cost: Provided after initial consultation

This service offers comprehensive design support for projects that modify your home's layout, whether through remodeling existing spaces or adding new rooms. Pricing is determined by square footage and project complexity. Following an initial consultation (billed at $150/hour), you'll receive a detailed scope of work and a fee proposal. The service includes:

  • Consultation and Site Measure
  • Two Conceptual Designs to choose from which include Space Planning and 3D Renderings
  • Furniture, Paint and Material Selections
  • Two Revisions, if needed
  • Ordering Services with trade discount (up to 20%)
  • Project Management
  • Installation and Designer Styling
